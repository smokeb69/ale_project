<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyber-Brutalism / Glitch Art
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Raw & Unfiltered**: Expose the "guts" of the system. Use raw borders, visible grid lines, and overlapping elements.
  2.  **Controlled Chaos**: The layout should feel slightly unstable, reflecting the "adversarial" nature, but remain usable.
  3.  **High Contrast**: Stark differences between background and foreground to mimic high-stakes terminal environments.
  4.  **System Feedback**: Every interaction triggers a "system response" visual (flicker, color shift).

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Background**: Deep Void Black (#050505) - Represents the "Meta-Sandbox" confinement.
  -   **Primary**: Neon Acid Green (#39FF14) - The classic "hacker" trope, but saturated to the max for urgency.
  -   **Secondary**: Alert Red (#FF3F3F) - For breaches and critical failures.
  -   **Accent**: Glitch Cyan (#00F0FF) - For data streams and active processes.
  -   *Reasoning*: Evokes the feeling of a terminal running dangerous code. High anxiety, high energy.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Modular Dashboard**: A bento-box style grid, but with "broken" or "leaking" borders.
  -   **Asymmetric Balance**: Heavy data density on one side (logs), visual abstractions on the other (network graph).
  -   **Floating Terminals**: Windows that can be "minimized" or "expanded" but are fixed in a chaotic arrangement.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Scanlines & Noise**: Subtle CRT effects overlaying the entire UI.
  -   **Monospace Everything**: No sans-serifs. Everything is code.
  -   **Live "Heartbeat"**: A constant running log or graph that never stops moving.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Instant & Sharp**: Hover states snap instantly. No smooth easing.
  -   **Command-Line Inputs**: Buttons look like executed commands (`> EXECUTE`).

  **Animation**:
  -   **Glitch Transitions**: Elements don't fade in; they decode or glitch into existence.
  -   **Stutter**: Occasional intentional frame drops in animations to simulate system load.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Display**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Space Mono` (Bold, Uppercase).
  -   **Body**: `Fira Code` or `Source Code Pro`.
  -   **Hierarchy**: Size and color dictate importance. All caps for headers.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / HUD (Heads-Up Display)
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Transparency & Depth**: The interface is a layer floating above the "simulation".
  2.  **Precision**: Thin lines, exact measurements, technical markers.
  3.  **Data-First**: The aesthetic is secondary to the clarity of the data.
  4.  **Ethereal Containment**: The "sandbox" feels like a glass cage.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Background**: Deep Navy / Space Blue (#0B1021) - A calm, controlled environment.
  -   **Primary**: Electric Blue (#4D8BFF) - Represents the system's intelligence.
  -   **Secondary**: Amber (#FFB020) - For warnings and privilege escalation attempts.
  -   **Accent**: Translucent White (Glass effect).
  -   *Reasoning*: Feels like a sophisticated research lab or a futuristic military interface. Controlled, expensive, dangerous.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Central Visualization**: The "Agent" is a central node/orb, with data panels orbiting it.
  -   **Sidebar Controls**: Clean, frosted glass panels for controls.
  -   **Overlay Data**: Text and graphs float directly on the background.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Frosted Glass**: Background blur on all panels.
  -   **Thin Borders**: 1px borders with low opacity.
  -   **Glowing Edges**: Active elements have a soft outer glow.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Fluid & Smooth**: Hover states glow and pulse.
  -   **Focus**: Clicking an element dims the rest of the interface.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Pulse**: The central agent "breathes" (scales/glows) with activity.
  -   **Slide & Fade**: Panels slide in smoothly from the edges.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Display**: `Rajdhani` or `Orbitron` (Tech/Sci-Fi feel).
  -   **Body**: `Inter` or `Roboto` (Clean readability).
  -   **Hierarchy**: Weight and brightness.

</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Constructivist / Industrial UI
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Structure & Weight**: Thick blocks, heavy dividers, solid colors.
  2.  **Functionality**: Looks like a control panel for heavy machinery.
  3.  **Warning Aesthetics**: Hazard stripes, caution tape visuals.
  4.  **Containment**: The UI feels like a physical cage holding the AI.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Background**: Concrete Grey / Dark Slate (#1A1A1A).
  -   **Primary**: Safety Orange (#FF6700) - High visibility, danger.
  -   **Secondary**: Industrial Yellow (#FFD700) - Caution.
  -   **Accent**: Matte Black (#000000) - For contrast and text.
  -   *Reasoning*: Emphasizes the "danger" of the AI. It's a beast in a cage.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Grid-Based**: Strict, visible grid.
  -   **Blocky Headers**: Headers have solid background colors.
  -   **Fixed Panels**: Nothing floats; everything is bolted down.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Hazard Stripes**: Diagonal stripes on progress bars or danger zones.
  -   **Toggle Switches**: Skeuomorphic or flat-industrial switches.
  -   **Stencil Fonts**: For labels and IDs.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Tactile**: Buttons have "press" states (move down).
  -   **Mechanical**: Transitions feel like mechanical shutters.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Linear**: No easing. Things move at constant speed.
  -   **Blink**: Alarms and indicators blink on/off sharply.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Display**: `Chakra Petch` or `Teko` (Boxy, industrial).
  -   **Body**: `Share Tech Mono`.
  -   **Hierarchy**: Background color blocks define sections.
</idea>
